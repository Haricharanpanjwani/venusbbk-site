Inquiry-first package strategy

Share package direction without locking in fixed public pricing

The earlier recommendation was right: for this kind of venue, it is better to invite package inquiries than publish rigid pricing that depends on date, guest count, menu, and decor complexity.

Flexible, lead-generating package page Show what can be tailored, then move the visitor into conversation

Wedding packages

Position this around stage decor, guest capacity, dining layout, and celebration support rather than a one-line flat rate.

Reception packages

Focus on ambience, hosting scale, decor, dining flow, and family movement.

Birthday & family packages

Present flexible setup, themed decor options, smaller guest formats, and food-service support.

Corporate hosting

Add meeting layout, stage or presentation support, seating structure, and hosted refreshments if relevant.

Useful package details to add later

  • Applicable event types
  • Seasonal or weekday offers
  • Catering inclusions or exclusions
  • Decoration tiers
  • Seating and stage setup options
  • Rooms or add-on services, if available

Best call to action

Encourage visitors to request a package on WhatsApp with their event date, type, and expected guest count. That matches how these bookings usually close.
